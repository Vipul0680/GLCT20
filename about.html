ABOUT GLC T20

Uniting Nations Through the Spirit of Cricket

Our Story

Global League Cricket (GLC T20) is an international T20 cricket league created to deliver world-class cricket, global entertainment, and meaningful sporting impact.

Bringing together international franchises, legendary cricketers, emerging talent, and passionate fans, GLC T20 is designed as a premium platform that celebrates the spirit of cricket while promoting tourism, culture, and global sporting collaboration.

Our vision is to build a modern cricket ecosystem that supports talent development, strengthens international partnerships, and creates unforgettable experiences for players, partners, and audiences worldwide.

With a strong focus on professionalism, innovation, and global reach, GLC T20 aims to become one of the most exciting and influential cricket leagues on the international stage.

VISION

To become a globally recognized T20 league that unites nations through cricket, entertainment, and cultural exchange.

MISSION

  • Promote international cricket development
  • Create opportunities for emerging talent
  • Deliver world-class fan experiences
  • Support tourism and global partnerships
  • Build a sustainable and professional model
